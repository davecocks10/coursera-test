Chicken

Chicken is a term which refers to the male of a number of poultry. It is raised in many places for its meat and eggs They are usually kept by humans as livestock. Some breeds of chickens can fly for a short distance. Some sleep in trees. A male chicken is called a rooster or a cock. A female chicken is called a hen.
